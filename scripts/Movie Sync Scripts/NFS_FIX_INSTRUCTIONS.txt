ðŸ“‹ WHY THIS HAPPENS
================================================================================

NFS mounts often don't allow chown/chmod operations, especially when:
  - Mounted with "all_squash" or "root_squash"
  - User doesn't have admin rights on remote filesystem
  - Synology has specific user/permission restrictions

The --no-perms --no-owner --no-group flags tell rsync:
  "Just copy the files, don't try to preserve ownership/permissions"

This is SAFE and CORRECT for this use case:

  âœ“ Content is identical
